A Bit More About Me

My first degree is in Philosophy, because I am really curious and I wanted to know the meaning of Life, the Universe and Everything (with dubious aid of boolean logic). I did my MSc in Managerial Economics at the London School of Economics, but broke free from the suit life and taught myself to code because being able to create things out of thoughts is so satisfying (the boolean logic finally helped). It's a great way to combine creativity with logic. My ambition is to be a master builder of the digital world.

My motto is "work hard and be nice to people". When I'm offline, I like to be miles from anywhere with everything I need in my backpack (sometimes for days). A good flat white, skiing powder and Christmas rock my world. But most of all, I love to learn.
